About Me

I am an Assistant Professor with School of Informatics, Xiamen University, China. Before that, I was a research engineer at Huawei (from Nov. 2016 to Dec. 2020). I received my PhD degree in Information Engineering at The Chinsese University of Hong Kong in 2016. Previously, I received my BSc and ME degrees in Computer Science from Nanjing University in 2009 and 2013, respectively.

My research interests are in wireless communication and networks, computer networks, and mobile computing. I am particularly interested in building practical systems that use state-of-the-art techniques to solve real-world problems. I am currently focusing on

  • Over-the-Air Computation (OFDM, CSS);

  • Low-Power Wide Area Networks (LoRa, NB-IoT);

  • Network Verification, Repair and Synthesis.
