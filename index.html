About Me

I am an Assistant Professor with School of Informatics, Xiamen University, China. Before that, I was a research engineer at Huawei 2012 Labs (from Nov. 2016 to Dec. 2020). I received my PhD degree in Information Engineering at The Chinsese University of Hong Kong in 2016. Previously, I received my BSc and ME degrees in Computer Science both from Nanjing University.

My research interests are in the area of wireless communication (e.g., IoT, 5G/6G), computer networks and systems (e.g., Data Center Networks, Wireless Transport), and mobile computing (e.g., localization, sensing). I like to build practical systems to solve real problems.

I am currently focusing on

  • Low-Power Wide Area Networks (LoRa, NB-IoT);

  • 5G vRAN (L1/L2 algorithms, platform);

  • Network Verification.

I am a member of Future Networking Lab@XMU led by Prof. Liqun Fu.
